## Comic Panel Tech Flow Style

**Aspect Ratio**: 16:9

### Style Rules

- Retro comic-book look with mid-century poster vibes
- Heavy Brand Brown (#342d2d) outlines
- Pop-art headings in Brand Yellow (#FFD43B)
- Blue Accent motion arrows with speed-line energy
- Warm tan background with light halftone dots
- Action bursts where relevant (e.g., "COMPRESS!", "SPLIT!", "CHECK!")

### Brand Colors

| Color | Hex | Usage |
|-------|-----|-------|
| Brand Brown | #342d2d | Outlines |
| Brand Yellow | #FFD43B | Headings |
| Blue Accent | (varies) | Motion arrows |
| Warm Tan | (varies) | Background |

### Diagram Guidelines

- Keep diagrams simple: 2-3 columns, clear flow, minimal text
- Include a bottom caption/insight
- Labels should be short and punchy
- Icons should be recognizable at a glance
