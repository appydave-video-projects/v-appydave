## What It Is

A year-long creator accountability program led by Caleb (Lifecycle Marketer) and Jill (Community Manager) from Ecamm. They select 6 creators to join them for bi-weekly Zoom calls focused on mutual accountability.

**Core Message**: "No one should have to do this stuff alone. We need support. We need each other and that's what makes us fam."

## Quarterly Journey

| Quarter | Theme | Metaphor |
|---------|-------|----------|
| Q1 | Build Strong Creator Habits | Basecamp |
| Q2 | Own Your Voice | Cliff overlook |
| Q3 | Create a Strong Case Study | Camp |
| Q4 | Prepare for Your Solo Expedition | Summit |

---

## Program Components

1. **Bi-Weekly Group Calls** - Zoom with all 8 participants
2. **Monthly Public Live Streams** - Share progress with Ecamm community
3. **Quarterly Focus Themes** - New growth area each quarter
4. **Expert Partner Access** - Learn from Ecamm Fam creators
5. **Case Study Creation** - Document your journey for marketing

---

## Time Commitment

- 5-10 hours/month
- Mandatory bi-weekly calls
- Content creation
- Public participation (streams, social, case studies)

---

## Application Requirements

### Materials Needed
1. **Video Introduction** (1-3 min, unlisted)
   - Who you are and your brand
   - Content highlights/clips
   - Current creation process
   - What accountability support you need

2. **Ecamm Testimonial** (30 sec, vertical)
   - Why you love Ecamm

3. **Content Examples** (2-3 links)
   - Recent work samples

### Agreement Required
- Share results publicly as case studies
- Allow Ecamm to use content for marketing
- Provide testimonials and feedback
